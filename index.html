Our Atelier

Where Art Meets
Precious Metal

Founded in Paris in 1998, Lumière has grown from a single craftsman's workshop into a globally celebrated jewellery house. Our commitment remains unchanged: every piece must be worthy of becoming a heirloom.

We source only ethically certified gemstones and use traditional goldsmithing techniques passed down through generations — updated with modern precision tools.
